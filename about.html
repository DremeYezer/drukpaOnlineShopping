About Us:


At Drukpas Online Shopping, our purpose is simple: To Re-define convenience!

There's this notion that to grow a business, you have to be ruthless. But we know there's a better way to grow. One where what's good for the bottom line is also good for customers. We believe businesses can grow with a conscience, and succeed with a soul and that they can do it with Drukpas Online Shopping.

With the aim to redefine Bhutan's shopping experience for our customers. We're creating an experience that lets customers shop anytime, anywhere - delivering exactly what they want, when and where they want it.

Drukpas Online Shopping is Bhutan's pioneering e-commerce shopping destination present through physical retail store, online portal and a mobile application. Our footprint currently extends to over 99 registered stores from every part of the country with diverse product portfolio ranging from electronics, IT equipment's, groceries, hardware tools, pet care, home appliances, automobile accessories, baby products, fashion, Bhutan lottery and Made in Bhutan products.

It's no secret, modern day life has time constraints as our world becomes increasingly connected and on the go. Thus, our digital team strives to be at the forefront of innovation, providing effortless and convenient solutions for our customers' needs. We are committed to developing experiences of the future by bringing the stores to our customers wherever they are and whenever they need us. We are on a mission of innovation through software and technology enhancements.

From the humble beginning as a software project, as a distinct legal organization today, Drukpas Online Shopping works to increase credibility and trust by continuing to look for creative methods to appeal to a broader, more digital generation of shoppers. We are transforming our business, one digital initiative at a time. We are redefining convenience at Drukpas Online Shopping.
